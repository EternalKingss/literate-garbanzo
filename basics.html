1. üí™ What is a "Comp" (Composition)?

Comp = Troop distribution: Infantry / Range / Cavalry.

Always scaled to 20 points.

Examples:

  • 659 = 6 Inf / 5 Range / 9 Cav
  • 866 = 8 Inf / 6 Range / 6 Cav
  • 4412 = 4 Inf / 4 Range / 12 Cav

Rule:

  • Always add parts to 20.
  • If not ‚Üí double or normalize.

7. üëë Basic Hero Knowledge

Hero Compositions:

  • 122 hero comp = 1 Infantry / 2 Range / 2 Cavalry (ideal for Range Phalanx)
  • 221 hero comp = 2 Infantry / 2 Range / 1 Cavalry (good for Infantry Phalanx)
  • 113 hero comp = 1 Infantry / 1 Range / 3 Cavalry (optimal for Cavalry Phalanx)

Must-Know Hero Facts:

  • Using only 1 Range hero in Range Phalanx is disastrous for positioning
  • Heroes not only boost stats but affect squad positioning on battlefield
  • F2P players should focus on Rose Knight, Prima Donna, and Trickster first
  • Hero tactical skills create positioning advantages during battles

Top F2P Heroes:

  • Rose Knight: #1 F2P hero, provides army attack boost
  • Trickster: Important for research boosts
  • Prima Donna: Excellent healing abilities
  • Demon Slayer: Great Infantry hero during wars
  • Tracker: Strong Range attack, good for monster hunting

1433 Composition Overview

The 1433 composition is a powerful infantry-focused formation used for rallies against specific enemy compositions. This guide will break down the troop counts, gear requirements, hero selection, and battle strategies for optimizing your 1433 rallies.

Key Advantage: The 1433 composition is mainly effective against enemy formations with low cavalry counts (under 1 million cavalry troops).

Troop Distribution for a 4.21M Rally

Infantry (14 parts)

2,510,000

(14/20) × 4.21M troops

Ranged (3 parts)

850,000

(3/20) × 4.21M troops

Cavalry (3 parts)

850,000

(3/20) × 4.21M troops

When to Use the 1433 Composition

The 1433 composition excels in specific battle scenarios and against certain enemy formations:

  • Effective against enemy formations with low cavalry counts (less than 1 million)
  • Particularly strong against 442 and 974 compositions
  • Works regardless of enemy gear or formation when their cavalry count is below the threshold
Important: Always scout your opponent before launching a 1433 rally. If they have more than 1 million cavalry troops, consider a different composition.

Formation Requirements

Infantry Phalanx

For 1433 composition, you must use Infantry Phalanx formation to maximize effectiveness

Key Notes:

  • Carpets are not necessary for this composition as long as you meet the troop requirements
  • The composition relies heavily on infantry stats, so focus your gear and talents accordingly
  • Always verify enemy cavalry count before launching the rally to ensure success

Hero & Familiar Selection

The 1433 composition uses full infantry heroes and familiars to maximize the power of your infantry troops.

If you don't have a specific infantry hero, make sure to replace it with another infantry hero of similar quality. Do not include more than 1 ranged or any cavalry heroes in this composition setup.

Gear Recommendations

For the 1433 composition, prioritize gear that boosts Infantry Attack, Infantry HP, and overall Army Attack stats.

Battle Strategy

When using the 1433 composition for rallies, follow these strategic steps:

  1. Scout enemy troops to confirm they have less than 1 million cavalry
  2. Set your formation to Infantry Phalanx
  3. Equip full infantry-focused gear, heroes, and familiars
  4. Fill the rally with the correct 1433 troop distribution
  5. Activate any relevant boosts or talents before launching
  6. Launch the rally and watch for victory!
Strategic Tip: The 1433 composition works best when the enemy doesn't expect it. Try to hide your rally composition until the last moment to prevent them from changing their formation or reinforcing with cavalry.

659 Composition Overview

The 659 composition is a balanced formation with a strong cavalry focus, making it versatile for various rally scenarios. This guide covers everything you need to know about setting up and executing effective 659 rallies.

Key Advantage: The 659 composition offers excellent versatility and is effective against a wide range of enemy comps that are mix.

Cavalry (9 parts)

1,600,000

When to Use the 659 Composition

The 659 composition is ideal in the following situations:

  • Particularly effective against 424 variants such as 947, 956, 857, 749, 965, and 866
  • When you need a versatile composition that works against a variety of defenses
Important Warning: Do NOT send a 659 against 884 or 992 , 055 compositions. Important Warning: 668 Also counters you so if you do see one coming get ready but as much as a 992 but it does have more advantage.

Formation Requirements

The formation you use for a 659 composition depends on the enemy's troop distribution:

Formation Selection Guide

For 659 composition, you have two primary formation options based on the enemy's composition:

  • Ranged Phalanx: Best against compositions heavy in range (like 866 with 6 parts range)
  • Cavalry Phalanx: Best against compositions low in range

Key Notes:

  • Analyze scouting reports carefully before deciding which formation to use
  • Using the wrong formation can significantly reduce your effectiveness
  • Balance between offensive and defensive stats is recommended for this composition

Hero & Familiar Selection

The 659 composition works best with cavalry-focused heroes and familiars to maximize your main troop type advantage.

Gear Recommendations

Other Gear options

  • For the main Hand you have 3 options Champion blade, Skullcrusher, Champain Edge
  • for the Off hand you can go with Champion light, Dragon fist, Book of the Dead, or Mitts

    • Battle Strategy

    When executing a 659 rally, follow these strategic steps for maximum effectiveness:

    1. Scout the enemy to verify their troop composition
    2. Set your formation based on enemy composition:
      • Ranged Phalanx for range-heavy enemies
      • Cavalry Phalanx for enemies low on ranged troops
    3. Fill the rally with the correct 659 troop distribution

    • Heavy range / Cav formations (like 884 or 992)

    Always adapt your strategy based on scouting reports and previous battle experiences against your target.

    866 Composition Overview

    The 866 composition is a powerful infantry-focused formation with balanced ranged and cavalry support, effective in specific rally scenarios. This guide covers the optimal setup and execution of 866 rallies.

    Key Advantage: The 866 composition excels against enemies with low cavalry counts and offers strong infantry presence with balanced support troops.

    Troop Distribution for a 4.21M Rally

    Infantry (8 parts)

    1,800,000

    (8/20) × 4.21M troops

    Ranged (6 parts)

    1,100,000

    (6/20) × 4.21M troops

    Cavalry (6 parts)

    1,100,000

    (6/20) × 4.21M troops

    When to Use the 866 Composition

    The 866 composition is most effective in the following situations:

    • Against enemy formations with low cavalry counts
    • Particularly effective against:
      • 974 compositions
      • 965 compositions
      • 884 compositions
      • 992 compositions
      • 712 compositions
    Important Warning: Do NOT use 866 against 659, 569, 758, 857, 208, or 055 compositions as these will counter your formation effectively. Always scout your target before launching.

    Formation Requirements

    Ranged Phalanx

    For 866 composition, always use Ranged Phalanx formation to maximize effectiveness

    Key Notes:

    • The Ranged Phalanx formation is mandatory for this composition to be effective
    • This formation helps protect your substantial infantry count while leveraging ranged attacks
    • Never use other formations with this composition as it will reduce effectiveness significantly

    Hero & Familiar Selection

    The 866 composition works best with infantry-focused heroes with some ranged support.

    Hero Tip: Balance your hero selection with an emphasis on infantry attack and health, while including 1-2 ranged heroes for support.

    Gear Recommendations

    For the 866 composition, prioritize gear that enhances Infantry Attack and Infantry Health with some Ranged Attack bonuses.

    Battle Strategy

    When executing an 866 rally, follow these strategic steps for optimal results:

    1. Scout the enemy to confirm they have a low cavalry count
    2. Set your formation to Ranged Phalanx
    3. Equip your infantry and ranged focused gear and heroes
    4. Fill the rally with the correct 866 troop distribution
    5. Coordinate with guildmates for proper support if needed
    6. Activate any relevant boosts or talents before launching
    7. Launch the rally and monitor results
    Strategic Tip: The 866 composition is particularly effective against defensive compositions with low cavalry counts. Use it as part of a strategic rally chain to maximize effectiveness against targets with mixed defenses.

    Counter Awareness

    Be aware that your 866 composition can be countered by:

    • Cavalry-heavy compositions like 659, 569, and 758
    • Formations with balanced troop distributions like 857
    • Special formations like 208 and 055

    Always verify enemy composition through scouts before committing to an 866 rally to avoid being countered.

    055 Composition Overview

    The 055 composition is a specialized formation with minimal infantry and balanced ranged/cavalry troops, designed for specific counter-rally scenarios. This guide explains how to correctly build and execute an effective 055 rally.

    Key Advantage: The 055 composition is extremely effective against enemy formations with low ranged troop counts, creating a powerful counter-attack opportunity.

    Troop Distribution for a 4.21M Rally

    Infantry (0 parts)

    50,000

    Exactly 50,000 troops only

    Ranged (5 parts)

    2,075,000

    (10/20) × 4.21M troops

    Cavalry (5 parts)

    2,075,000

    (10/20) × 4.21M troops

    When to Use the 055 Composition

    The 055 composition should be used in very specific scenarios:

    • Against enemy formations with less than 1.1 million ranged troops
    • As a specialized counter against infantry / Cav-heavy compositions
    • When the precise troop counts can be maintained (exactly 50,000 infantry)
    Important Warning: Do NOT use 055 against compositions with more than 1.3 million ranged troops. You could potentially win against an enemy with 1.3 million range if they have 2 infantry heroes, but if they have only 1 infantry hero, avoid using this composition completely.

    Formation Requirements

    Infantry Wedge

    For 055 composition, always use Infantry Wedge formation regardless of enemy formation

    Key Notes:

    • It is critical to maintain exactly 50,000 infantry troops - no more!
    • The Infantry Wedge is the only formation that works with this composition
    • The balanced ranged and cavalry components are essential for this composition's effectiveness

    Hero & Familiar Selection

    The 055 composition requires a specialized hero selection to maximize effectiveness.

    Hero Tip: Balance between ranged and cavalry heroes for maximum effectiveness. Avoid using more than one infantry hero in this composition.

    Gear Recommendations

    For the 055 composition, focus on gear that enhances Ranged and Cavalry stats equally.

    Battle Strategy

    When executing an 055 rally, follow these strategic steps for success:

    1. Scout the enemy to confirm they have less than 1.1 million ranged troops
    2. Check if the enemy has 2 infantry heroes (beneficial) or just 1 (avoid if they have 1.3M+ range)
    3. Set your formation to Infantry Wedge
    4. Verify you have exactly 50,000 infantry troops - this is critical!
    5. Fill the remaining rally with equal parts ranged and cavalry (approximately 2.075M each for a full rally)
    6. Launch the rally when ready, maintaining your formation
    Strategic Tip: The 055 composition is highly specialized and should only be used in the right circumstances. When used correctly, it can deliver devastating results against opponents with low ranged troop counts.

    Counter Awareness

    Be aware that your 055 composition can be countered by:

    • Formations with more than 1.3 million ranged troops
    • Opponents with more than one infantry hero and high ranged counts
    • Compositions specifically designed to counter mixed ranged/cavalry attacks

    Always verify enemy composition through scouting before committing to an 055 rally to avoid severe losses.

üéÆ Retargeting Mechanics

Understanding how heroes affect squad positioning and targeting is crucial for optimizing battle performance.

How Heroes Impact Squad Positioning

Heroes fundamentally change how squads behave in battle through their tactical skills and positioning effects.

Theoretical Explanation: Hero Tactical Skills

  • The difference in squad position is caused by the hero's tactical skill
  • When activated, it has a slightly longer reach than an ordinary squad attack
  • This keeps the squad position a little bit further away compared to a squad unattended by any hero
  • The activation is characterized by the enlargement of the hero figure on the field
  • When the hero figure shrinks, that's the finish point of the longer reach, followed by a position correction
  • When squads attended by heroes face each other, both activate at similar timings, maintaining relative position
  • If only one side has a hero, the squad without the hero makes an extra step toward the opposing squad
  • This applies to any troop type, not just ranged heroes

Visual Example of Squad Positioning:

In a battle, each hero creates a "zone of control" around their assigned squad that alters its positioning:

  • Squads with heroes stay slightly further back (safer position)
  • Squads without heroes advance further forward (exposed position)
  • This positioning difference can lead to targeting advantages or disadvantages
  • The effect compounds when comparing armies with different hero distributions

Hero Distribution Impact:

Different hero distributions create different squad formations on the battlefield:

  • Balanced Distribution: Even protection across all squad types
  • Front-loaded Distribution: Better protection for front-line troops
  • Specialized Distribution: Enhanced performance for specific troop types
Strategic Tip: Understanding hero positioning effects is one of the most advanced mechanics in Lords Mobile. This knowledge can help you defeat opponents with significantly higher might.

üõ°Ô∏è Hero Gear

Hero gear selection dramatically affects both individual hero performance and overall battle effectiveness.

Hero-Specific Gear

Different heroes benefit from different gear sets based on their role and abilities.

Infantry Hero Gear Focus:

  • Twilight Priestess: Focus on Infantry ATK/HP gear with Army bonuses
  • Oath Keeper: Maximize Infantry DEF stats and Army HP
  • The Big Guy: Balance Infantry ATK with Army ATK gear

Range Hero Gear Focus:

  • Petite Devil: Prioritize Range ATK and Army ATK
  • Snow Queen: Focus on Range ATK with some Range HP
  • Grove Guardian: Balance Range ATK with Army stats

Cavalry Hero Gear Focus:

  • Barbarian: Maximum Cavalry ATK and Army ATK
  • Storm Fox: Focus on Cavalry ATK and Army HP
  • Child of Light: Balance between Cavalry and Army stats

Gear Set Strategies:

  • Multiple Sets: Develop different gear sets for different battle scenarios
  • P2P Options: Champion and Mythic gear offer the highest stat bonuses
  • F2P Options: Focus on event gear, Hell Events, and Guild Fest rewards
  • Jewels: Infantry/Range/Cavalry ATK jewels offer the best value for their specific troop types

Stat Priority for Heroes:

Different scenarios may require different stat priorities:

  • Rally Leader: Balance ATK and HP stats for sustained performance
  • Defending: HP stats generally provide better survival value
  • KvK Points: ATK stats maximize kills and point generation
Strategic Tip: Having specialized gear sets for different heroes allows you to quickly adapt to different battle scenarios without needing to reconfigure your gear each time.

Familiar Activation Timing

Understanding familiar activation timing is crucial for maximizing their effectiveness in battle.

Activation Thresholds:

  • Early Activation (4% Troops): Jade Wyrm, Blackwing, Gargantua, Bon Appeti
  • Later Activation (8% Troops): Queen Bee, Frostwing, Tidal Titan
  • Multiple Activation: Queen Bee and Frostwing skills activate up to 5 times

Activation Impact:

Different activation timings can dramatically affect battle outcomes:

  • Early activation familiars help establish initial advantage
  • Sustained damage familiars (Queen Bee/Frostwing) excel in longer battles
  • Burst damage familiars can instantly eliminate key enemy squads
  • Familiar abilities can change which enemy squads retarget to which of your squads
Strategic Tip: Jade Wyrm is considered the most valuable Pact 5 familiar due to its universal counter boosts and damage reduction that benefit any composition and formation.

Development Priority

Developing familiars requires careful resource allocation and priority setting.

Development Order:

  1. Pact 3 Resource Familiars: Gemming Gremlin and Goblin should be prioritized for their passive resource generation
  2. Pact 1 Utility Familiars: Aquiris and Territe provide immediate value for a small investment
  3. Pact 4 Key Familiars: Hoarder and Mecha Trojan offer unique benefits worth investing in
  4. Pact 5 Combat Familiars: Jade Wyrm, Blackwing, and Queen Bee for battle optimization

F2P Familiar Focus:

  • Daily Activation: Focus on familiars that provide daily bonuses first
  • Resource Management: Resource-generating familiars help sustain growth
  • Contract Balance: Balance anima use between combat and utility familiars
  • Event Value: Save puzzle pieces for events that provide additional rewards

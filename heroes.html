2. üéÆ Heroes' Role in Retargeting

Heroes play a significant role in determining the retargeting sequence in battles.

Retargeting Explained:

Retargeting in Lords Mobile refers to the change in the target squad receiving damage. Retargeting occurs only when the current squad being attacked is completely killed.

For example, if an INF squad attacks an opposing ranged squad, it can only transition to attacking another enemy squad once the ranged squad is completely killed.

The Issue with One Range Hero:

  • With one range hero, the ranged squad in the middle (unattended by a hero) sticks out towards the enemy
  • The opposing ranged squad (attended by the ranged hero) sticks out away from the opponent
  • Enemy INF squads ignore the range squad in the middle and move backwards
  • This mechanism creates an inherent disadvantage

Examples of Battle Outcomes:

Scenario Result
4-4-2 with two ranged heroes vs. 749 ranged phalanxes Easy win for the 4-4-2
4-4-2 with one ranged hero Loss
4-4-2 with zero ranged heroes Terrible loss
9-5-6 with two ranged heroes vs. 4-4-2 ranged phalanxes Easy win for the 9-5-6
9-5-6 with one ranged hero vs. 4-4-2 ranged phalanxes Nearly impossible to win unless statistically favored
Three ranged heroes in ranged phalanxes Induces a harmful mechanism similar to having one ranged hero

Theoretical Explanation: Hero Tactical Skills

  • The difference in squad position is caused by the hero's tactical skill
  • When activated, it has a slightly longer reach than an ordinary squad attack
  • This keeps the squad position a little bit further away compared to a squad unattended by any hero
  • The activation is characterized by the enlargement of the hero figure on the field
  • When the hero figure shrinks, that's the finish point of the longer reach, followed by a position correction
  • When squads attended by heroes face each other, both activate at similar timings, maintaining relative position
  • If only one side has a hero, the squad without the hero makes an extra step toward the opposing squad
  • This applies to any troop type, not just ranged heroes

Key Stat Balancing:

Both Attack and HP are equally important for different scenarios:

  • Multiple rallies: Focus on HP to reduce losses
  • Point earning (KvK): Focus on Attack stats
  • Overall combat effectiveness: Balance both

Counter-Intuitive Stat Effects:

  • Increasing HP can sometimes cause more damage to the opponent
  • Army Attack can sometimes reduce losses more than the same amount of HP
  • Increasing Attack can sometimes worsen results (e.g., in 442 vs 659 Range Phalanx)
  • Higher Attack can trigger retargeting problems when it kills enemy Range too quickly

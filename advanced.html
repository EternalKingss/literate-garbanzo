üéØ Wedge Formation Tactics

Understanding how wedge formations change battle dynamics is critical for advanced players.

Range Wedge vs Range-heavy enemies (e.g., 7112):

  • Spreads Infantry wider
  • Makes Cavalry stay back longer
  • Result: Cavalry survives longer, joins battle later when Range squads are already softened
  • Range Wedge ruins 7112 comp mechanisms because Infantry takes longer to collapse

Infantry Wedge vs Cavalry-heavy enemies:

  • Wider Infantry spread stalls Cavalry charges longer
  • Gives Range squads more time to clean up

Hero Impact on Wedge Formations:

  • For Range Wedge - 2 Range heroes are essential to maintain proper squad positioning
  • For Infantry Wedge - Oath Keeper and Twilight Priestess enhance effectiveness significantly
  • Heroes with tactical skills create more spread when in Wedge formations
Strategic Tip: Wedge formations are often underestimated but can completely counter certain compositions when used correctly.

üîç Retargeting Mechanics

Retargeting in battle is a critical mechanic that decides who wins in mirror matchups.

Important: In many 4-2-4 vs 4-2-4 Range Phalanx battles, whoever gets their Range killed FIRST often gains battle dynamics in their favor.

When Range squads are eliminated:

  • The timing of Range destruction heavily influences battle outcome
  • Killing enemy Range faster can sometimes be a disservice
  • If both Range are killed simultaneously, expect a close match

Hero Impact on Retargeting:

Heroes play a significant role in determining retargeting sequences - this is why the 2 Range Hero rule is so critical.

  • Hero Tactical Skills: When activated, have slightly longer reach than ordinary squad attacks
  • Squad Positioning: Squads with heroes stay slightly further back compared to unattended squads
  • Positioning Impact: With 1 Range hero, your middle Range squad sticks out toward the enemy
  • Retargeting Result: Enemy Infantry squads ignore the Range squad and move backward, creating a major disadvantage

Examples of Hero Impact on Battle Outcomes:

Scenario Result
4-4-2 with two Range heroes vs. 749 Range Phalanx Easy win for the 4-4-2
4-4-2 with one Range hero Loss
4-4-2 with zero Range heroes Terrible loss
Strategic Tip: The 2 Range hero rule applies to almost all compositions that use Range Phalanx formation. This is non-negotiable for optimal performance.

üß™ 992 and Special Compositions

Special compositions like 992 are designed for specific battle scenarios and counter strategies.

The 992 composition is special because:

  • Cavalry acts as buffer against Infantry Blast/Phalanx
  • Almost always requires carpeting
  • Frequently used in Emperor and Baron Wonders
  • Intended to counter 424 variants in Range Phalanx
Why not use 550? The 992's Cavalry serves as protection for its Infantry after the 442 winning mechanism is induced. It reduces casualties while still getting reasonable kills.

992 With Optimal Heroes and Familiars:

  • Hero Setup: 122 hero comp (1 Infantry, 2 Range, 2 Cavalry) maximizes 992 effectiveness
  • Top Heroes: Petite Devil and Grove Guardian (Range), Storm Fox and Barbarian (Cavalry), Oath Keeper (Infantry)
  • Familiar Strategy: Jade Wyrm for counter boost, Blackwing for its powerful attack, Queen Bee for sustained damage
  • Formation Choice: Almost always Range Phalanx for 992 compositions

‚öîÔ∏è Attack vs HP Stats

Both Attack and HP stats are equally important in different battle scenarios.

  • HP reduces your losses
  • Attack increases damage to opponents
  • Their synergy creates robust outcomes

Strategic Considerations:

  • Multiple Rallies/Reduced Losses: HP might be more suitable
  • Earning Points (KvK, etc.): Attack stats might be better
  • Increasing Win Chance: Both Attack and HP are ideal

Counter-Intuitive Stat Effects:

  • Increasing HP can sometimes cause more damage to the opponent
  • Increasing Attack can sometimes worsen results in specific matchups (like 442 vs 659 Range Phalanx)
  • Army Attack can sometimes reduce losses more effectively than equivalent HP stats
  • Stats influence familiar activation timing, which can dramatically shift battle results
Strategic Tip: Specialized rally leaders often have different gear sets optimized for Attack vs HP depending on the battle scenario and objectives.

  • Familiar choice should complement your formation and counter the enemy's expected setup
